# 📦 Supply Chain Analytics Project

> End-to-End Supply Chain Analytics Project using **Excel, SQL, Python, and Tableau** developed as part of the **Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI) – Data Analytics Track**.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates a complete Supply Chain Analytics workflow, starting from raw data preprocessing and ending with interactive Tableau dashboards that support business decision-making.

The project focuses on analyzing financial performance, supplier efficiency, inventory health, logistics operations, and customer returns to generate actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall financial performance.
- Evaluate supplier performance.
- Monitor inventory health.
- Optimize logistics operations.
- Analyze customer returns.
- Support data-driven decision-making.

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Customer Satisfaction
- Inventory Volume
- Stockout Risk
- Supplier Reliability
- On-Time Delivery
- Shipping Cost

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Achieved a **53.37% Profit Margin**, indicating strong profitability.
- The **North Region** generated the highest revenue.
- **Online Sales** was the highest-performing sales channel.
- **Supplier 2** achieved the highest profit contribution with the shortest lead time.
- **DHL** recorded the highest on-time delivery performance.
- Product defects were the primary driver of customer returns.
- Inventory optimization can significantly reduce stockout risk.

---

# 🚀 Recommendations

- Optimize inventory management to reduce stockout risk.
- Improve product quality to minimize returns.
- Focus on high-performing suppliers.
- Balance shipping cost with delivery performance.
- Continuously monitor KPIs through interactive dashboards.
